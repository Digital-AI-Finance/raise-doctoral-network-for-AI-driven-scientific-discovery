What is RAISE DN?

RAISE Doctoral Networks train a new generation of researchers in AI-driven scientific methods across all disciplines. Part of the EUR 107M RAISE pilot under Horizon Europe.

Who Can Apply?

Consortia of at least 3 independent entities from 3 different EU/Associated Countries. AI must be integral and indispensable to the research programme.

Funding Per Researcher

~EUR 8,420/month (max): EUR 4,250 living allowance + EUR 710 mobility + EUR 660 family + EUR 2,800 institutional costs.

Data sourced from the official Horizon Europe Work Programme 2026-2027 and MSCA Work Programme 2026-2027.
