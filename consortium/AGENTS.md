## For AI Agents

### Working In This Directory
- Consortium rules: min 3 independent entities from 3 different EU MS/Associated Countries
- At least 1 entity must be in an EU Member State
- Max 40% of EU contribution to same-country beneficiaries
- China exclusion applies (legal entities in China NOT eligible)
- Target consortium size: 8-12 partners for competitive proposal
