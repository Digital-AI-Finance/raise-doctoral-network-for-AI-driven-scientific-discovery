## CRITICAL ISSUES (Must Fix)

### C1: No Unifying Scientific Question
- "AI for Finance" is a technology area, not a research programme
- Network reads like 15 independent projects
- Need a testable thesis, e.g. "Can causal AI systems replace equilibrium-based models?"
- Intellectual glue between clusters is thin

### C2: 4 DCs Fail RAISE "Integral and Indispensable" Test
- **DC10 (ESG Greenwashing):** Multi-modal classification is established, not architecturally novel
- **DC3 (Multi-Modal Financial AI):** Cross-modal attention is not new in 2026; domain application ≠ AI innovation
- **DC15 (Parametric Insurance):** Integration/engineering work, not fundamental AI methodology
- **DC13 (Concept-Based XAI):** Concept bottleneck models exist (Koh 2020); regulatory framing = domain application

### C3: Methodology Descriptions Are Aspirational
- "Novel message-passing schemes" and "new score-matching objectives" are promissory notes
- No concrete algorithmic innovations described
- Cannot evaluate feasibility without specifics

### C4: Training Programme Completely Missing
- No network-wide training events
- No transferable skills programme
- No secondment strategy
- No description of how DCs gain BOTH AI AND finance competencies
- MSCA DN is a TRAINING action first

### C5: No Consortium Named
- No institutions, supervisors, track records, complementarity analysis
- Cannot assess feasibility, geographic/sectoral balance, 40% cap
- No hosting arrangements described

### C6: No Work Plan
- No work packages, deliverables, milestones, risk assessment
- No Gantt chart
- Concept-note stage, not proposal stage

### C7: No Data Access Strategy
- Financial data is proprietary, expensive, regulated
- Assumes access to HFT data, bank exposures, satellite imagery, ECB supervisory data
- Data access is #1 practical risk — not mentioned
